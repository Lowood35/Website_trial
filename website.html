Welcome to Lowood Inn

Established in 2004, Lowood Inn offers a peaceful stay just minutes from the iconic Dal Lake. Nestled amidst serene surroundings, the property combines comfort with a homely charm. Guests can enjoy spacious rooms, warm hospitality, and easy access to Srinagar’s local attractions. Perfect for travelers seeking a cozy retreat with modern amenities in the heart of Kashmir.

Stay just steps away from Srinagar’s most treasured landmarks, including the Mughal Gardens, Tulip Garden, Botanical Garden, Pari Mahal, and Nehru Park.
